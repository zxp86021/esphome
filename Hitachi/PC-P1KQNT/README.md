### Register Chart

Resgister Address | Item | Resgister Value
----------------- | ---- | ---------------
0x00 | Current Temperature  | Celsius (Read Only)
0x20 | Power                | 0: OFF<br>1: ON
0x21 | Ventilate Mode       | 1: Auto<br>8: Heat Exchage<br>16: Normal
0x22 | Fan Speed            | 2: High<br>4: Low
